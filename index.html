Fanpage : Tokyo Ghoul/Re

Welcome to my fanpage.

Here you can find some information about my favorite manga: Tokyo Ghoul (and Re).

Tokyo Ghoul is a manga created by Sui Ishida and focuses on the conflict between ghouls and humans.

I like Tokyo Ghoul because it has a really beautiful and well-thought-out story that many people overlook.

I heavily suggest reading Tokyo Ghoul if you're into tragedy, psychological thrillers, and fantasy.

This is a GOATED manga and my TOP 1 above One Piece, Baki, JJK, and Dr. Stone.
